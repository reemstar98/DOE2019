Microsoft Teams 

Microsoft Teams is a digital hub that brings conversation, content and apps together in one place all from a single experience in Office 365.

Educational Use

Use to store files and collaborate with team members such as your stage, faculty or professional learning community. Teams also allows you to create and upload files that can be accessed on any internet-connected device. Students can use Teams for group work, project-based learning and so on. Teachers can schedule, upload and track assignments.

Learn more

For an online tutorial, visit Microsoft's Teams video training.

The online course, Microsoft Teams Essential Training (staff only), is available through Lynda.com.

Search for courses and resources at the Microsoft Educator Community.

For assistance see Microsoft's Teams Get Started.

Get Started

Log in to your department Office 365 account using a web browser for access. Select ‘Explore all your apps’ to find Teams and install the Teams desktop app.
